Biography

I'm currently a first year Computer Science PhD student at UC Santa Barbara, advised by Prof. William Wang. My work is about natural language processing using deep learning.

I received BSE from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2017, and worked as a research assistant in NTU from 2017 to July 2019. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Hung-yi Lee, Prof. Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen, and Prof. Lin-shan Lee.

For more details, you can check my CV/resume. If there is any question, please feel free to contact me via email at pascaltuan@gmail.com / ytuan@ucsb.edu
